  • Vocational and Skills Training
  • Public Speaking 
  • Time Management
  • In spite of the various limitations faced by the School, De La Salle College has always been striving to keep abreast with new developments through the support of its Old Boys, Parents and Well-Wishers. As part of its social responsibility, the school initiated a Peace Education Programme aimed at creating communal harmony among the younger generation at a time when the county is enveloped in an ethnic war. Pilot projects were also done in Leadership Development and Social Integration.

“INDIVISA MANENT”

De La Salle College, Colombo is the only school in Sri Lanka dedicated to the Patron Saint of Christian Teachers St. John Baptist de la Salle, founder of the congregation of De La Salle Brothers of the Christian Schools in Latin: "Fratres Scholarum Christianarum".
